How to Play

1

Power flows from the top

Energy enters the board from the Power Source. Your job is to get it to the bottom.

2

Rotate blocks to connect

Tap any block to rotate it 90° clockwise. Align the conductive cells to form a continuous path.

3

Circuit complete!

Energy reaches the Circuit Ground — watch the board light up with a satisfying glow.
