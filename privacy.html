Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-02-27

SeepCell ("the App") is a free mobile puzzle game developed as an independent project. This policy explains what data is collected and how it is used.

1. Data We Collect

The App itself does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All game progress (scores, unlocked levels, settings) is stored locally on your device only.

2. Advertising (Google AdMob)

The App displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use data to serve personalised or non-personalised ads. This data may include:

  • Device identifiers (Advertising ID)
  • IP address and approximate location
  • App interaction data
  • Device and OS information

You will be asked for your consent before personalised ads are shown (where required by law). You may withdraw consent or opt out of personalised advertising at any time through your device settings.

For more information on how Google uses data, please visit:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

3. Children

The App is rated for general audiences (PEGI 3 / Everyone). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. Ad requests are configured to comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

4. Third-Party SDKs

The only third-party SDK integrated into the App is Google AdMob. No analytics, crash reporting, or social SDKs are included in this version.

5. Data Security

All game data is stored locally on your device. No data is transmitted to our servers (we operate no servers). AdMob data handling is governed by Google's security practices.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your region, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete personal data held about you. Because we do not collect personal data directly, requests should be directed to Google for AdMob-related data.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy when new features are added. The effective date at the top of this page will be updated accordingly.

8. Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at:
privacy@seepcell.com
